# Sundanese Artifacts Images

Place your Sundanese artifact images in this folder with the following filenames:

## Required Images:

1. **keris-sunda.jpg** - Keris Pusaka Sunda (traditional dagger)
2. **angklung-gubrag.jpg** - Angklung Gubrag (large bamboo musical instrument)
3. **topeng-klana.jpg** - Topeng Klana (wooden mask from Cirebon)
4. **kujang.jpg** - Kujang Sunda (traditional weapon)
5. **gamelan-degung.jpg** - Gamelan Degung (traditional music ensemble)
6. **wayang-golek-cepot.jpg** - Wayang Golek Cepot (wooden puppet)
7. **batik-kawung.jpg** - Batik Kawung Priangan (traditional fabric)
8. **golok-ciomas.jpg** - Golok Ciomas (traditional machete)
9. **kendang-pencak.jpg** - Kendang Pencak (drum for martial arts)
10. **tenun-lurik.jpg** - Tenun Lurik Sunda (woven fabric)
11. **karinding.jpg** - Karinding Bambu (bamboo mouth harp)
12. **badak-beureum.jpg** - Badak Beureum ceramic container

## Image Specifications:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Recommended size: 800x600px or higher
- Aspect ratio: 4:3 or 3:2 preferred
- File size: Under 500KB for better performance

## Sources for Images:
- Museum collections (Sundanese museums in West Java)
- Cultural heritage websites
- Free stock photo sites with Indonesian cultural content
- Wikipedia Commons (Sundanese artifacts)
